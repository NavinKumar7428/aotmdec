Satellite Internet Companies

Leading global and Indian companies driving satellite-based connectivity and digital inclusion.

Global Players Indian Initiatives

Global Players


Starlink (SpaceX)

Starlink operates a large Low Earth Orbit (LEO) constellation delivering high-speed, low-latency internet worldwide. It focuses on rural connectivity, maritime, aviation, and emergency services.

  • LEO satellite constellation
  • Low latency broadband
  • Rapid deployment



OneWeb

OneWeb focuses on enterprise, government, and aviation connectivity using a global LEO network. It plays a major role in connecting remote and underserved regions.

  • Enterprise-grade services
  • Global coverage
  • Strategic partnerships



Amazon Project Kuiper

Project Kuiper is Amazon’s upcoming LEO satellite network aimed at providing affordable broadband to unserved and underserved communities globally.

  • Affordable broadband vision
  • Large-scale satellite deployment
  • AWS integration potential

Indian Initiatives


ISRO & NSIL

India’s space agency supports satellite communication through GSAT satellites, enabling broadcasting, broadband, disaster response, and rural connectivity.

  • GSAT communication satellites
  • Disaster management support
  • National digital infrastructure



Tata Group & Airtel (OneWeb Partner)

Indian telecom giants collaborate with global satellite providers to deliver enterprise-grade and rural connectivity solutions across India.

  • Enterprise and rural focus
  • Strong ground infrastructure
  • Government & enterprise reach
